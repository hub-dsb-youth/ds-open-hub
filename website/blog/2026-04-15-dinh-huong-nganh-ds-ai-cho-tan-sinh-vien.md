Neu ban la tan sinh vien dang hoc Data Science hoac AI, chac chan ban se co nhieu cau hoi:

- Mon nao quan trong nhat?
- Ky nang nao can co de di duong dai?
- Minh co hop voi nganh nay khong?

Bai viet nay tong hop theo cach de hoc, de lam, de duy tri dong luc.

## Ba truc cot loi

### 1) Toan

Toan la nen tang cua ML and DL, khong phai phan phu.

Can uu tien:

- Dai so tuyen tinh
- Giai tich
- Xac suat thong ke
- Toi uu hoa (convex optimization)

Khi toan chac, ban se:

- Hieu duoc ban chat du lieu
- Hieu tai sao model dung hoac sai
- Biet cach debug va cai tien model

### 2) Tech and Tools

Bat dau tu nhung gi rat co ban nhung phai chac:

- Python: doc, viet, debug
- SQL: truy van va lay du lieu dung cach
- pandas and numpy: xu ly data
- matplotlib and seaborn: truc quan hoa
- scikit-learn: modeling co ban

Sau khi vung, mo rong sang:

- PyTorch hoac TensorFlow
- Power BI
- Git and GitHub workflow

Loi khuyen quan trong: dung bo qua data cleaning and transformation. Day la phan tao chenh lech lon giua nguoi moi hoc va nguoi lam duoc viec.

### 3) Domain

Data khong tu sinh ra insight neu ban khong hieu ngu canh.

Vi du voi domain tai chinh, ban nen hieu mot so khai niem nhu RSI, MACD, thanh khoan, xu huong gia va risk.

Nguoi moi nen chon chu de tao dong luc va su to mo, roi di sau dan theo huong domain minh yeu thich.

## Ai phu hop voi nganh nay?

- Co kha nang phan tich van de
- Co tinh tu hoc cao
- To mo theo nghia tich cuc: ben bi, dam thu, dam sai, dam sua
- Co hung thu voi du lieu va cong nghe

To mo la yeu to rat quan trong. No giup ban break down bai toan lon thanh bai toan nho va kien tri giai den cung.

## Lo trinh de xuat (ban improved)

### Giai doan 1: 0-3 thang

- Cung co Python and SQL
- Lam 2 bai EDA nho
- Hoc Git co ban

### Giai doan 2: 4-6 thang

- Hoc stats and feature engineering
- Lam classification and regression
- Viet README ro rang cho project

### Giai doan 3: 7-9 thang

- Hoc dashboard and storytelling
- Chon 1 domain de dao sau
- Tap trung metric and business insight

### Giai doan 4: 10-12 thang

- Hoc Deep Learning co ban
- Lam 1 capstone co demo
- Chuan hoa portfolio 3-5 project

## Loi nhan gui

Ban khong can gioi ngay tu dau. Ban can mot lo trinh ro rang, su ben bi, va thai do hoc hoi lien tuc.

Chuc ban hoc tot, tien bo deu, va giu duoc su to mo voi nganh nay.
